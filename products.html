Mauritius Hemp

By Baghban

Mauritius hemp is a large, evergreen succulent that can reach up to 12 feet in height and 15 feet in width. It produces tall spikes of white flowers and can tolerate drought, heat, and poor soil conditions. It's a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions and is used for fiber production and has some medicinal uses.
