Functional Specification

  1. Overview: The plant and landscaping business will offer a variety of plants, flowers, and landscaping services to residential and commercial customers. The business will aim to provide high-quality products and services to customers who are looking to enhance their living or working spaces with greenery and plant life.
  2. User interface: The business will have a website that serves as the primary interface for customers to browse and purchase products and services. The website will have an easy-to-navigate design, with clear sections for plants, landscaping services, and decorations.
  3. Plant inventory: The business will maintain an inventory of various plant species, including but not limited to flowers, succulents, tropical plants, and trees. The inventory will be regularly updated to ensure that customers have access to the latest plant products.
  4. Landscaping services: The business will offer various landscaping services, including garden design, installation, and maintenance. Customers can request a consultation with a professional landscaper to discuss their specific needs and receive a customized landscaping plan
  5. Decorations: The business will provide decorations for special events, such as weddings, parties, and corporate events. Customers can choose from various floral arrangements and centerpieces to enhance the aesthetic appeal of their events.
  6. Custom-made statues: The business will also offer custom-made statues made of different materials such as clay and cement, which customers can use to decorate their gardens, patios, or living spaces.
  7. Customer service: The business will provide excellent customer service to ensure customer satisfaction. Customers can contact the business via phone or email with any questions or concerns they may have about products or services.
  8. Payment and shipping: Customers can purchase products and services on the website using various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Shipping and delivery options will be available for customers to choose from based on their location.
  9. Security: The website will be secured with the latest security measures to protect customer information and ensure safe transactions.
  10. Technical requirements: The website will be built using modern web development technologies and will be optimized for fast loading and mobile responsiveness. The website will also be hosted on a reliable server with sufficient resources to handle traffic and provide a smooth browsing experience for customers.
