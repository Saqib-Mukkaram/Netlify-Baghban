About Us

Welcome to Baghban!
At Baghban, we are dedicated to bringing the beauty of nature into your life. In the field of plants, we provide a wide range of products and services for different kinds of flowers as well as indoor plant. We have designed our platform to be a onestop shop for all plant related needs. Our team in Baghban has a passion for plants and gardening, which is why they strive to provide you with the best quality products and services. A diverse range of species, each selected to be beautiful, health and sustainable, are included in our careful selection of plants. We have something for everybody, whether you are an experienced gardener or a newcomer looking to plant some herbs in your home or office. Besides our wide range of products, we are offering a number of services to help you create your ideal garden oasis. We offer professional gardeners who can advise you on your plants, maintain them and look after them so they survive in their new environment. We're offering you the opportunity to add a touch of style and artistic charm to your space by providing decorative services, such as hanging pots or custom sculptures. We're proud to provide our customers with outstanding customer service in Baghban. Our team will always be on hand to assist you with any queries or concerns, and we are grateful for your feedback that helps us continuously improve our services. We pledge to provide you with the highest level of security, payment options and protection of personal data while shopping on this website. We care aboutthe sustainability of the environment and give back to our community. We're sourcing our plants from certified organic growers, encouraging ecological best practices and working actively with local initiatives and charities that are dedicated to plant conservation and protecting the environment. Thank you for choosing Baghban's as the place where all your plant needs will be met. We're looking forward to being part of your journey and creating a beautiful, green world around you!
Sincerely,
The Baghban Team


Our Vision

Our mission at Baghban is to encourage and facilitate people's connections with nature and the creation of vibrant green areas that improve both their quality of life and the environment. We want to be a market leader in the plant sector, renowned for our superior quality, extensive product selection, and first-rate customer service. Our goal is to establish ourselves as a go-to source for plant lovers, gardeners, and nature lovers alike, offering them the inspiration, skills, and resources they need to nurture their passion for plants and design stunning, environmentally friendly settings. We expect Baghban to be a platform offering not only an extensive selection of plants and related products but also professional guidance, training as well as support for our customers. We are trying to create a space where plant lovers can gather, learn and share their love of plants in an environment that promotes the sense of connection and belonging. In addition, in all aspects of our business we will seek to raise sustainability and awareness about the environment. We're going to set an example for our sourcing practices, packaging and manufacturing processes, minimising the environmental impact while encouraging choice of eco sustainable products. We're also committed to joining with likeminded organizations and initiatives, which will have a positive impact in the protection of our planet's resources and biodiversity. Our vision aims to make Baghban a symbol of trust, quality and excellence within the plant industry. By helping people connect to nature, beautify their spaces, and contribute to a greener and more sustainable world, we aim to make a positive impact on people's lives.
Join us on this journey towards a thriving, green future with Baghban!
